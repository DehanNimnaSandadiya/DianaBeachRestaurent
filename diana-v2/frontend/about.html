About Diana Beach

A family restaurant on Matara's coastline, serving authentic Sri Lankan flavours to guests from around the world since 2005.

Our Story

Born from the Sea

Diana Beach Restaurant was established in 2005 by the Fernando family on the sun-drenched shores of Matara. What began as a small beachside café serving fresh catches of the day has grown into one of the most beloved dining destinations on the southern coast.

Our kitchen is led by chefs who grew up along these very shores, bringing generations of culinary wisdom to every dish. We source our seafood daily from local fishing boats, and our spices come from farms just inland.

As Sri Lanka became a global travel destination, we realised our guests came from every corner of the world — each with different tastes and culinary backgrounds. That insight inspired us to build a smarter way to help every visitor find what they'd love most.

Our Innovation

Nationality-Based Food Recommendation

We believe every guest deserves a dining experience that feels made just for them.

🌍

Cultural Intelligence

Indian guests may love spicy fish curries while British visitors often prefer milder grilled seafood. Our system learns these patterns from real reviews.

📊

Bayesian Algorithm

We use a weighted Bayesian scoring model — the same approach as IMDb's Top 250 — combined with recency decay for accurate recommendations.

🔄

Always Improving

The recommendation engine updates in real-time as new reviews arrive, ensuring suggestions stay current and relevant.
