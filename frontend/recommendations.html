The Algorithm

How Our Recommendation Engine Works

Powered by a weighted Bayesian scoring model — the same approach used by IMDb's Top 250 ranking.

📝

1. Guests Leave Reviews

After dining, guests rate dishes 1–5 stars and share their nationality.

⚖️

2. Bayesian Averaging

Raw averages are adjusted toward the global mean when review counts are low, preventing new dishes with one 5-star review from dominating.

📅

3. Recency Weighting

Recent reviews carry more weight using exponential decay — a dish that was great last week matters more than one from two years ago.

✨

4. Personalised Results

The top-scoring dishes for your nationality are presented in ranked order, with full transparency on the scoring breakdown.
